run_pair = α·max(A_run,B_run) + (1-α)·min(A_run,B_run)
wicket_pair = α·min(A_wk,B_wk) + (1-α)·max(A_wk,B_wk)
Notes: wicket values are "wicket_run_impact" (lower is better; pairing rewards the *better* wicket profile). Z-scores are computed vs the baseline distribution in baseline.json.
